DRAWING

1. How did you learn how to draw?

I set the personal challenge of drawing the human face and used all types of free-knowledge sources available on the internet such as free books, Youtube videos about various mediums (pencils, pens, oil, pastels and digital) and communicating with other artists making questions about their techniques on their Instagram or Facebook pages. But most importantly: persistance. Drawing 40 minutes per day was also part of the challenge.

2. What are your materials? 

My graphite pencils range from HB to 8B, an eraser, an eraser-pencil, a blender and a hard bristles brush, that's all. You don't need to get fancy to draw. I use several brands for pencils but I don't have a favorite one. Brand X can have very hard pencils, but hard pencils are great for textures so there's no such thing as a "bad brand". You can get some glimpses of my materials on my Instagram page.

DRAWING SERVICES

3. I'd like to make my hair longer, my nose smaller and change my lips for Angelina Jolie ones. Can you do it?

Yes, I can, but I won't. I'll follow STRICTLY what your picture shows so I recommend that you choose a picture taken by a professional photographer to be used as reference. Some photographers do physiognomy change services and you can find Photoshop freelance professionals online so this can be sorted before the commission is set.

4. Do you accept commissions bigger than A3?

Yes, but their pricing and drawing time is different, send me a message via the contact form on the home page about your prefered size. For smaller papers such as A4, the price will be exactly the same as the ones shown on the pricing page due to shipping, so I don't recommend it.

5. Do you frame your drawings?

No. The product you will receive is a drawing on a sheet of paper along with the package details you can see below. And because the paper isn't water proof, can get dirty and may fold depending on the way it is handled after you open your package, I recommend that you have a way of framing it settled before the commission arrives.

6. What are "progress pictures"?

Every business day after your commission is set, you'll receive via e-mail pictures of the progress of the drawing so you know how it is taking shape, as it builds reliability and trust between the artist and the client. When the drawing is finished, the final pictures will be sent for approval so I know you're happy with the results before I send the drawing. 7. Can I cancel my order?

You can't cancel your order after you receive the first e-mail with the first progress pictures, these pictures will arrive within 24 hours of the next business day after the commission details are set. If I already started the drawing, I won't cancel your order and neither give back the 50% paid beforehand.

For example: if you set your commission on a Friday night, you have until Sunday night to cancel your order, as the progress pictures may arrive at any moment on Monday.

If you set your commission on a Wednesday at any time, you have until the end of the day 23:59 S√£o Paulo time to cancel your order, as the progress pictures may arrive at any moment on Thursday. 

SHIPPING

8. When will my commission arrive?

As soon as your order ships, you will receive an e-mail with tracking and delivery information. Every type of commission has a specific number of business days to be finished and this can be checked at the PRICING page. For the shipping time, please add from 20 to 25 days for your purchase to arrive in you home country. If the drawing will be used to celebrate an important date or it'll be a wedding/birthday gift, please keep these informations in mind.

9. What shipping service do you use?

I use Brazil's national mail service called Correios for free shipping. If you'd like to receive your commission via FedEx, DHL or UPS, this must be set before payment, as additional costs will apply.

10. How will my commission be packaged?

The drawing will have one sheet of paper on the front and another one on the back, they will be inside a clear plastic bag, this clear plastic bag will be between 2 sheets of styrofoam, while other 2 sheets of MDF boards will keep everything else from bending and transportation damage. The items will be held together with packing paper and duct tape.

11. Can I send my order directly to a gift recipient?

Certainly - just inform me the address beforehand. You may also leave a printed gift message free of charge, send me the file and I'll print it in A5 size, kindness is free ;)
