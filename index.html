PAYMENT

You can pick any of the payment processor below and the invoices will be sent via e-mail.
Payment is done in two steps: 50% before the commission starts and 50% after the commission ends and the final result is approved. After both invoices are paid, the drawing will be shipped.

Paypal

The world's largest payment gateway.

You'll receive two e-mails containing the invoices when the commission details are set. Invoice clients are also under Paypal's buyer protection.

Bitcoin

10% discount for Bitcoin clients. Let's make Bitcoin mainstream.

The amount of BTC will be set on the purchase day, based on the commission's price with the discount and the USD conversion. You'll receive an e-mail with the Bitcoin address for both payments.
